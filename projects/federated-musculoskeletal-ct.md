# Federated Learning for Quantitative Musculoskeletal CT Analysis

Research on privacy-aware collaborative learning for segmentation and quantitative analysis of musculoskeletal structures in CT.

## Focus

- Multi-institutional model training without centralizing patient images
- Robustness to scanner, protocol, cohort, and annotation differences
- Reliable downstream muscle, adipose tissue, and bone measurements

## Status

Ongoing research. Methods, results, and publication links will be added as they become available.
