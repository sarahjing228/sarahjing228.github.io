# CT-based Swallowing and Oropharyngeal Structure Analysis

Quantitative analysis of anatomical structures associated with swallowing and oropharyngeal function using CT imaging.

## Focus

- Segmentation of relevant muscles and soft-tissue structures
- Reproducible morphometric and attenuation measurements
- Associations between imaging features and functional or clinical outcomes

Research scope in development. More details will follow as the project progresses.
